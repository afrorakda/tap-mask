# #35 8 Rounds Tabata

A hyper-minimalist HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) timer designed for the Tabata Protocol. No distractions, just the rhythm to push your limits.

## ⚡️ 1 Tool / 1 Action
- **One-Tap Start**: Pre-set to the global standard (20s Work / 10s Rest / 8 Sets). Open and hit START.
- **Visual Cue**: The background shifts between **White** (Rest) and **Light Gray** (Work), allowing you to track your session through peripheral vision while training.
- **Auto-Save**: Remembers your custom intervals and set counts automatically via LocalStorage.
- **iOS Optimized**: Fluid performance on Safari with dedicated text-fill fixes for clear visibility.

## 🛠 How to Use
1. Open the tool.
2. (Optional) Tap the numbers to quickly overwrite your desired intervals. (Auto-select on tap)
3. Press **START** and focus on your performance.

## 🛡 Privacy First
- **No Login**
- **No Tracking**
- **No Ads**
- **Browser-only**
